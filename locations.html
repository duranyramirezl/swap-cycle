The Swapshop

Address: Haarlemmerdijk 89, 1013 KC Amsterdam

The Swapshop promotes a sustainable lifestyle by encouraging people to trade their clothes instead of buying new ones. Through their garment-swapping system, they offer an eco-friendly alternative to fast fashion, helping to extend the lifespan of clothes and reduce waste.

THE UPCYCLE / Cream Denim

Address: Sint Annendwarsstraat 13, 1012 HC Amsterdam

THE UPCYCLE collaborates with Cream Denim to transform discarded materials into high-quality, sustainable denim pieces. By upcycling old garments, they reduce waste and showcase innovative, eco-conscious fashion solutions for a more sustainable wardrobe.

Kringloop Amsterdam

Address: Kerkstraat 354, 1017 JA Amsterdam

Kringloop Amsterdam is a well-known thrift store with several locations that encourage the reuse of secondhand items, including clothing. By offering a wide range of pre-loved garments, they help reduce waste and support a circular economy in Amsterdam.
