Flared Jeans

What You'll Need

  • ✤ A pair of slim/skinny jeans
  • ✤ Scissors
  • ✤ A piece of fabric (ideally denim or another sturdy material)
  • ✤ Pins
  • ✤ Sewing machine (or needle and thread for hand-stitching)

Steps

  1. Cut Along the Seam: Lay your jeans flat and decide where you want the flare to start. Usually, just above the knee works best. Carefully cut along the outer seam from the bottom hem up to this point.
  2. Measure and Cut Your Fabric: Measure the length of the cut on your jeans. Use this length to cut two triangular pieces from your fabric, making sure they’re wide enough at the bottom to create a noticeable flare.
  3. Pin the Fabric: With your jeans inside out, pin each triangular piece of fabric to the open seams, making sure the widest part of the fabric is at the hem.
  4. Sew It Up: Sew along the edge where the jeans and fabric meet, removing pins as you go. Reinforce the seam with a backstitch at the top and bottom.
  5. Trim and Finish: Trim any excess fabric inside, turn the jeans right-side out, and enjoy your new flared look!
